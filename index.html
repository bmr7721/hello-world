Services

What I Do

  • 01

    PPC / SEM

    It can help a brand grow by driving targeted traffic to the website, generating leads, and increasing...

    Read More

    This is a type of advertising where businesses pay a fee each time someone clicks on their ads. Ads are placed on search engine results pages or other websites, and the goal is to drive traffic to the business's website.

    PPC/SEM can help a brand grow by driving targeted traffic to the website, generating leads, and increasing conversions. By using targeted keywords, businesses can reach people who are actively searching for their products or services.

    This can result in increased brand awareness, website traffic, and ultimately, sales.

  • 02

    Display Advertising

    Display advertising can help a brand grow by increasing brand visibility and driving traffic...

    Read More

    This is a type of advertising that uses images, videos, or other graphics to promote a product or service. Display ads can be placed on websites, social media platforms, or other digital channels.

    Display advertising can help a brand grow by increasing brand visibility and driving traffic to the website. By using eye-catching graphics and targeting options, businesses can reach a wider audience and increase brand awareness and website traffic.

  • 03

    Social Media Advertising

    Social media advertising can help a brand grow by reaching a highly targeted audience...

    Read More

    This is a type of digital advertising that is displayed on social media platforms such as Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn. Social media advertising can be used to target specific demographics, interests, and behaviors.

    Social media advertising can help a brand grow by reaching a highly targeted audience and driving engagement and conversions.

    By using targeting options such as location, demographics, interests, and behaviors, businesses can reach the right people with their ads and increase brand awareness, engagement, and conversions.

  • 04

    Email Marketing

    Email marketing can help a brand grow by building relationships with customers and generating...

    Read More

    This is a marketing strategy that involves sending promotional messages to a targeted audience via email. The goal is to build relationships with customers, generate leads, and increase sales.

    Email marketing can help a brand grow by building relationships with customers and generating repeat business.

    By sending relevant and personalized emails to subscribers, businesses can stay top-of-mind with their audience, increase engagement, and ultimately drive sales.

  • 05

    Native Advertising

    Native advertising can help a brand grow by blending in with the surrounding content and providing...

    Read More

    This is a type of advertising that blends in with the surrounding content and is designed to look like editorial content. The goal is to provide useful information to the audience while promoting a product or service.

    Native advertising can help a brand grow by blending in with the surrounding content and providing valuable information to the audience. By promoting their products or services in a non-intrusive way, businesses can build trust with their audience and ultimately drive conversions.

  • 06

    Graphic Design

    Effective graphic design helps brands stand out from competitors, communicate their values ...

    Read More

    Graphic design is an essential element of a brand's visual identity and can help a brand communicate its message and values to its audience. A well-designed logo, website, or marketing collateral can make a brand stand out and create a lasting impression on the audience.

    Effective graphic design helps brands stand out from competitors, communicate their values and personality, and create an emotional connection with their audience. It is a powerful tool for building brand recognition, enhancing brand reputation, and ultimately driving business growth.

    Graphic design can also help a brand convey its unique personality, establish credibility, and increase brand recognition and loyalty. With the right design elements, a brand can effectively communicate its message and differentiate itself from its competitors, ultimately helping it to grow and succeed in today's competitive market.

    Format releases a new tool that enables direct video hosting

    Just because we can't get out and about like we normally would, doesn’t mean we have to stop taking pictures. There’s still plenty you can do, provided you're prepared to use some imagination. Here are a few ideas to keep you shooting until normal life resumes.

    Most photographers love to shoot the unusual, and you don’t get much more unusual than These Unprecedented Times. Right now everything counts as out of the ordinary. There are a number of remarkable things about these lockdown days that are worth photographing now so we can remember them when it is all over.

    Most photographers find it hard to see interesting pictures in places in which they are most familiar. A trip somewhere new seems always exactly what our photography needed, as shooting away from home consistently inspires us to new artistic heights.

    Streets empty that are usually busy are remarkable and can evoke the sense of historical pictures from before the invention of the motorcar. Other things that are different at the moment will be queues to get into stores and the lines marked out on the floor to show how far apart we should be.

    Pretend everything is new and that you haven’t seen it before, and then you will be free to notice the leading lines, the places where one edge meets another in delightful geometric harmony, and how the ordinary things in the kitchen are transformed when the light is on or off.

    The trick here is to look slowly, and then look again. Take the time to look in detail and to look at the same thing from different angles, with different light, long lenses and wide lenses. Then move to the left a bit. You may never feel the need to leave the house again.
